Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

Our mission is to work together to build a safe,respectful and nurturing environment focused on maximizing each child's sense of well-being and acquisition of skills for life and learning.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to help learners acquire,demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners to participate in and contribute to the nation and the global world as well.

Our History

1985: Founding Years

Established with just 50 students in a small building, focusing on progressive education methods.

2002: Campus Expansion

Moved to our current 10-acre campus with modern science labs and sports facilities.

2020: International Recognition

Awarded "Best Innovative School" by the Global Education Network for our STEM programs.
