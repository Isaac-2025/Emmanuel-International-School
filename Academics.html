School Curriculum

Our curriculum combines Ghana Education Service standards with international best practices.

We employ student-centered learning approaches including:

  • Project-based learning
  • Collaborative group work
  • Differentiated instruction

Continuous assessment comprising:

  • Class tests (30%)
  • Projects (20%)
  • Mid-term exams (20%)
  • End-of-term exams (30%)
